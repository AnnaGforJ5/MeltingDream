Made by: Anna, Erik and Alan

Project for Interaction Design I, Spring 2024

This short game inspired by 'Five Nights at Freddy's' was built to work with a custom Makey Makey controller built to resemble the console in the game.

If you aren't using it, you're doing something wrong.

Development Process Documentation, accessible by East Bay accounts.

MakeyMakey Controls: Doors are controlled by the top three blue buttons. Cameras are controlled by the red and bottom two blue buttons.

The interior controls are used when the power turns off. Hold the green aligator clip for the current.

Keyboard Controls: Doors on Left Right and Up arrows, Spacebar for Cameras, W & A for changing cameras, SDFG for colors
